Illinois

Illinois is most known for its largest city, Chicago. While Chicago is cool, Southern Illinois is home to some beautiful forests. The capital of Illinois once was the home to the 16th president of the United States, Abraham Lincoln. Now visitors can tour his home, his grave, and visit the library built in his honor.
