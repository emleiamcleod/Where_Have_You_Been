Iceland

Iceland become an independent nation on June 17th 1944. I visited in October 2017. If you have the chance to go to Iceland the Hallgrímskirkja church is on the must visit list. Outside the church a monument of Leif Erickson can be found. If you are looking for somethign outside the city, the Drowning Pool, or Drekkingarhylur, has an intresting history and some beautiful scenory.
